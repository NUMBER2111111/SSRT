The Architecture

Triple-Write Durability

Default, not optional. Every commit writes three times.

  • - Primary: Canonical structured form - your source of truth
  • - Shadow: Redundant copy in separate location/device/partition
  • - Ledger: Append-only human-readable index + content pointers

Result: If primary fails, shadow takes over. If both fail, ledger rebuilds everything.

Intent Objects
